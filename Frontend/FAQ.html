Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, ordering, shipping, and more.

What types of products do you offer?

We offer a variety of farm-fresh products, including free-range chicken eggs, pasture-raised goats, and other seasonal farm goods. Our selection varies based on availability and the time of year, ensuring you always receive the freshest products.

How do I place an order?

Placing an order is simple. Browse our online shop, add your desired products to the cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll be prompted to enter your shipping and payment information. We accept various payment methods for your convenience.

What are your shipping options and costs?

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs, including standard and expedited delivery. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and the weight of your order. We strive to provide affordable and reliable shipping.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we generally do not accept returns. However, if you are unsatisfied with your purchase for any reason, please contact our customer support within 24 hours of delivery, and we will do our best to resolve the issue.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via the contact form on our website or by emailing us directly at support@Native Bites.com. We typically respond within one business day.
